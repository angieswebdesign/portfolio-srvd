Payments experience

Case Study

Overview

Florida Blue members manage their insurance coverage through the digital member portal, where billing and payments are critical tasks. As part of the visual design team, I collaborated with interaction designers and engineers to refine the presentation of payment information using the existing design system. My focus was on improving visual hierarchy, component consistency, and the visibility of key billing actions so members could quickly understand their payment status and access the tools needed to maintain their coverage.

  • Designed a dashboard component highlighting current payment status and billing actions
  • Improved visual hierarchy for billing and payment actions
  • Strengthened UI consistency across payment states

Context & Challenge

The payments experience plays an important role in how members manage their coverage and renew their plans. While interaction flows were defined by the interaction design team, the visual interface required refinement to make billing information easier to scan and act on. Members needed clearer visibility into their current balance, payment history, and available payment actions directly from the main dashboard.

Working within the existing design system, I focused on strengthening the visual structure of payment information and designing a highly visible dashboard component that surfaced the member’s current payment status.

Design considerations

  • Maintain consistency with the existing design system and component library
  • Surface critical billing information in a highly visible dashboard location
  • Improve scannability of balances, payment status, and actions
  • Ensure designs aligned with engineering constraints and implementation patterns
Result

Introducing a payment snapshot component in the dashboard hero area allowed members to immediately see their current balance and access the payment center without navigating deeper into the application.

Reflection

Designing the payments experience reinforced how critical clarity and consistency are when users manage important tasks like insurance coverage and billing. When user interfaces clearly communicate status, next steps, and confirmation states, members are able to complete transactions with greater confidence and trust in the platform.

“Clear, system-driven interface design can turn complex account management tasks into confident, everyday actions.”
